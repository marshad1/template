This time, though, Jim turns the alarm up and sits up for the first time rubbing the sleep out of his eyes and getting ready to start the day. He jumps in the shower before sitting down to have a bowl of cereal and a cup of coffee while perusing the newspaper. After he finishes his cereal and his cup of jo, he gets into his car and speeds away to work. Once Jim arrives, he is greeted by many of his coworkers and they quietly chat amongst themselves as they get ready to get to work as chemical engineers.
